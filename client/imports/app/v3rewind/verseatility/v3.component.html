VerseAtility III: Rewind
This documentary features rare footage from the heyday of Virginia based Frequency Magazine, shot in the 7 Cities of VA, Oakland, CA and NYC. V3Rewind's all-star line-up includes Questlove, Stretch Armstrong and Bobbito Garcia, Chad Hugo of the Neptunes, Jean Grae, Breeze Evahflowin, HorrorCity, Rubar & more. Enjoy interviews and dope performances of these cultural icons up close and personal as well as live on stage.
