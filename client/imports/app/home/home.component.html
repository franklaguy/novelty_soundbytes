Introduction  

Six Strange Tales is a complex and intertwined series of stories intricately woven around the fates of a small community of characters. Quentin Tarantino and Vincent Gallo come to mind as the plot twists and turns through alternating time periods in the lives of these anti-heroes, sadists, monsters and villains. This book may be the best noir literary work published in recent history.
